Fangs & Wings

2D Survival Game — 2025

About the Game

Fangs & Wings is a 2D survival game centered around dual-character control, where the player manages both a fox on the ground and an eagle in the air. The objective is to survive against continuously attacking bats that target the fox.

While the fox must be carefully moved to avoid danger, the eagle acts as the primary defense, eliminating enemies before they reach their target.

How to Play

  • Manage dual characters simultaneously — control the fox on the ground and the eagle in the air
  • Carefully move the fox to dodge enemy attacks
  • Use the eagle to attack and eliminate continuous waves of bats
  • Coordinate your characters to survive as long as possible
  • Adapt to the consistent pace of enemy spawns

Key Features

  • Dual-character control system requiring coordination and multitasking
  • Fixed interval bat enemy waves
  • Engaging survival gameplay loop targeting quick reflexes
  • Developed in C# leveraging the Unity Game Engine framework
