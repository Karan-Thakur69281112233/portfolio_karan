Boomerang

3D Obstacle Game — 2025

About the Game

Boomerang is a 3D obstacle game where players must cross a river using stepping stones. The game focuses on timing-based gameplay — players need to carefully time their jumps to land on moving platforms and avoid falling into the water.

Built entirely in Unity with C#, the game features realistic physics interactions, smooth controls, and progressively challenging levels that test the player's reflexes and timing skills.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  • Time your jumps carefully to land on stepping stones
  • The stones move at different speeds — watch the pattern!
  • Reach the other side of the river to complete each level
  • Each level increases in difficulty with faster and more complex patterns

Key Features

  • Timing-based stepping stone mechanics with varying speeds
  • Physics-driven player movement using Unity's Rigidbody system
  • Multiple difficulty levels with progressive challenges
  • Smooth third-person camera follow system
  • Water splash effects and environment particles
  • Score tracking and level completion system
